## Clinical Coverage

### Core ED Presentations
- **Cardiac**: ACS (NG185), Chest pain (CG95), Acute heart failure (CG187)
- **Neurological**: Stroke/TIA (NG128), Head injury (NG232), Meningitis (NG240), Epilepsy (NG217)
- **Trauma**: Major trauma (NG39-40), Spinal injury (NG41), Fractures (NG37-38, CG124)
- **Respiratory**: Asthma (NG245), COPD exacerbation (NG114), Bronchiolitis (NG9)
- **Sepsis**: Adult (NG253), Paediatric (NG254), Pregnancy (NG255)

### Resuscitation (RCUK 2025)
- Adult BLS and ALS algorithms
- Paediatric BLS and ALS algorithms
- Choking management
- Anaphylaxis algorithms
- Special circumstances

### Procedural Guidelines (RCEM)
- Pain management (adult and paediatric)
- Procedural sedation
- Fascia iliaca block
- Ketamine sedation

### Mental Health & Safeguarding
- Self-harm (NG225)
- Delirium (CG103)
- Alcohol disorders (CG100, CG115)
- Violence/aggression (NG10)
- Child safeguarding (NG76, CG89)

## Curation Criteria

### Included
- Acute presentations commonly seen in ED
- Time-critical conditions requiring ED management
- Paediatric emergencies
- Mental health emergencies
- Antimicrobial prescribing for acute infections
- Resuscitation and procedural guidance
- Safeguarding guidance

### Excluded
- Cancer management (not acute ED care)
- Long-term condition management
- Rehabilitation guidelines
- Prevention/screening programs
- Service commissioning guidance
- Developmental disorders (autism)
- Non-acute pregnancy management
